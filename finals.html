Popular Destinations

Banana Island

A secluded paradise with pristine beaches and crystal clear waters, perfect for snorkeling and relaxation.

Barracuda Lake

This unique lake in Coron features varying water temperatures and is a popular spot for diving enthusiasts.

Honda Bay

A perfect destination for island hopping, featuring beautiful islands and rich marine life.

Sabang Beach

Known for its proximity to the Puerto Princesa Underground River, this beach offers stunning views and relaxation.

Twin Lagoon

Two stunning lagoons separated by a limestone wall, accessible by swimming through a small opening during low tide.

Twin Lagoon View

Breathtaking views of the Twin Lagoon's crystal clear waters and towering limestone cliffs.

Underground River

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this 8.2 km navigable underground river features stunning limestone formations.

Big Lagoon

A natural wonder in El Nido, the Big Lagoon features towering limestone cliffs and emerald waters perfect for kayaking.
