Palawan

The Philippines' Last Frontier

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Palawan, voted as the World's Best Island multiple times. With pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning limestone formations, Palawan offers unforgettable adventures for every traveler.

Must-Visit Destinations

El Nido

Discover the stunning limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and pristine beaches of El Nido. Island hopping tours will take you to breathtaking spots like the Big and Small Lagoons, Secret Beach, and Seven Commandos Beach.

Northern Palawan 4.8/5

Puerto Princesa Underground River

Explore one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature. This 8.2 km underground river flows directly into the sea, and visitors can take a boat tour through its impressive cave system with stunning stalactites and stalagmites.

Puerto Princesa 4.7/5

Exciting Activities

Island Hopping

Explore the hidden beaches, lagoons, and snorkeling spots around El Nido and Coron.

Diving

Discover colorful coral reefs and diverse marine life in Palawan's crystal-clear waters.

Trekking

Hike to Mt. Tapyas in Coron or explore the jungles around the Ugong Rock formation.

Food Tour

Sample local Filipino cuisine and fresh seafood at beachside restaurants.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

November to May offers the best weather, with December to February being the peak season. Avoid the rainy months from June to October.

Getting There

Fly to Puerto Princesa, El Nido, or Coron from Manila. Puerto Princesa has the most flight options. From there, take vans or buses to other destinations.

Accommodation

From luxury resorts to budget hostels, Palawan offers accommodations for every budget. Book in advance during peak season.

Budget

Daily budget: $30-50 for budget travelers, $100-200 for mid-range, and $250+ for luxury experiences.
